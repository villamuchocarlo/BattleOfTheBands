Sponsors

Battle of the Bands would not be possible without the help of all of its sponsors. In previous years, we have been sponsored by many major brands, including Tempur, Sky Cable, Havaianas, Starbucks, and more. The sheer scale of the event means that it is only because of the money or sponsors donate that BOB can follow through at all! These resources also serve to increase the amount of aid we can provide toward our service partners, including PREP and SPECS. Our sponsors also provide half the fun at the event itself. Vendors sell the food and drinks needed to keep our energy up, and our other stands give the audience of BOB another place to look for opportunities.

Anyone who is interested in helping can get sponsors for the Battle of the Bands! Simply contact a member of the BOB committee, and we can make sure that your sponsor will be able to do their part.
